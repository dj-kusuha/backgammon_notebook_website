Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Backgammon Notebook mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "this Application" or the "Service").

1. Information Collection and Use

Our goal is to limit the collection of your personal data to what is strictly necessary to provide and improve the Service. We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, provide core functionality, and serve advertisements.

Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics)

This Application utilizes Google Firebase for backend infrastructure, authentication, and error reporting.

  • Firebase Authentication: If you choose to log in using external providers (such as Google or Apple), we will collect and store your authentication identifier (UID) to link your account data. By default, we do not store your name or profile picture in our Firestore database.
  • Cloud Firestore: Any backgammon board positions, match settings, and personal notes you create within the app are stored in Cloud Firestore, securely linked to your account.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: In the case of an error or app crash, we collect data and information (through Crashlytics) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within this Application. AdMob may collect the following information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads:

  • Your device advertising identifier (such as Apple's IDFA or Android Advertising ID).
  • Usage data and device information.

You can manage your advertising preferences or opt out of personalized tracking through your device's privacy settings.

2. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Please note that the third-party service providers used by this Application have their own Privacy Policies. We recommend reading their policies to understand how they handle your data:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Policies
  • Firebase Privacy & Security

3. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the account management screen within this Application. Upon executing this action, all of your saved positions, notes, authentication data, and account associations will be permanently removed from our databases.

Please note: The numerical results of backgammon position analyses (processed via the GNU Backgammon engine) are stored anonymously in our system based solely on the mathematical board position (gnubg ID). This analysis data contains no personal information, cannot be linked back to you, and will remain in our database for shared use even after your account is deleted.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
